Eco-Tips

Small changes, big impact. Start your sustainable journey here.

Tip #1: The 5 R's

Go beyond recycling! Remember to **Refuse** what you don't need, **Reduce** what you do, **Reuse** what you can, **Rot** (compost) your food scraps, and then **Recycle** what's left.

Tip #2: Energy Vampires

Unplug electronics when you're not using them. Chargers, coffee makers, and game consoles use "phantom power" even when they're turned off, wasting energy and money.

Tip #3: Eat Local

Buying from local vendors (like the ones on MossPay!) drastically reduces the carbon footprint from long-distance transportation, refrigeration, and packaging.

Tip #4: Save Water

Take shorter showers. A 5-minute shower uses 10-25 gallons of water. Cutting your shower time by just two minutes can save up to 10 gallons of water each time.
